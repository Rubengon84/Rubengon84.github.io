The Break Point

I studied Human resources at the university and I focused my career in-office staff and operational managing, I was working nearly 10 years combining office works and managing people in an export company when I realized that it was boring and monotone for me, I did not want to keep doing that all my life. So I moved to London to accomplished one of my dreams, to learn English. It was difficult at the beginning because I did not understand much English and I had to start in a lower position in the hospitality industry where my English level was not the most important but I am very grateful to all of them how gave me an opportunity and gave me the possibility of stay in London and let me improve my English and my life.

The New Beggining

My Journey in Web development began with a historical World event, The Covid19 pandemic, March of 2020 when the frighten Covid19 virus arrived and spread to most of the world. Many countries declared a lockdown being affected in many businesses and jobs everywhere. I had a stable job in an important restaurant in London and after the situation of the pandemic, the hospitality business as other industries were closed. Like other people, I found myself without a job and with an uncertain future. Instead of being depressive and frustrated with this situation, I turned my point of view and I started to see things in a positive way.
